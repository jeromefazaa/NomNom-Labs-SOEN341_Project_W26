- Project Description: 
MealMajor is a web application that helps university students take the stress out of meal planning. Users create a personal account, set their dietary preferences and allergies, build and manage a recipe library, and organize their week by assigning meals to specific days and meal types. The app also includes a daily macros and calories calculator, giving students a clear picture of their nutritional intake based on their planned meals. By centralizing everything in one place, MealMajor removes the daily friction of deciding what to eat, helping students save time and stay on top of their nutrition. The project is developed following Agile principles, with features delivered incrementally across multiple sprints.
